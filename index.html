House Signatures

Special Dry Noodles (Mì Khô Đặc Biệt)

A flavorful bowl of dry noodles tossed in our signature rich and savory sauce, delivering a perfectly balanced taste in every bite. The dish is generously topped with BBQ pork, shrimp, liver, quail eggs, and fresh greens for added texture and freshness.

Served with a side of hot, comforting broth, this dish brings together savory, slightly sweet, and aromatic flavors - perfect for those who love traditional dry noodles with a more premium, satisfying twist.
